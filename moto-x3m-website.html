About Moto X3M Pool Party

Moto X3M Pool Party is a thrilling motorcycle racing game that takes the popular Moto X3M series to a whole new summer-themed environment. Dive into the pool party atmosphere as you race through challenging tracks filled with water obstacles, diving boards, and exciting jumps.

Perfect your timing to execute flips and tricks that will reduce your overall time and earn you more stars. The faster you complete each level, the better your score will be. With intuitive controls and physics-based gameplay, Moto X3M Pool Party offers an addictive gaming experience for players of all skill levels.

Challenge yourself to complete all levels with a perfect 3-star rating as you master each track's unique layout and obstacles. Can you become the ultimate pool party motorcycle champion?

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your motorcycle
  • Press Up Arrow or W to accelerate
  • Press Down Arrow or S to brake and reverse
  • Use Left Arrow/Right Arrow or A/D to control your balance
  • Complete each level as quickly as possible to earn more stars
  • Perform backflips or frontflips to reduce your time
  • Try to find the optimal path through each level
